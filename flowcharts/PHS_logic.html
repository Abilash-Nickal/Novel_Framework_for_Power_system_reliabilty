The Chronological Dependency

Unlike standard Monte Carlo, this code cannot be fully vectorized. The amount of water available in the reservoir at Hour 500 (ep_mwh) strictly depends on whether the system charged or discharged at Hour 499.

Markov Pre-computation

To keep the script fast, generate_annual_states() is called outside the hourly loop. It mathematically pre-computes 8760 hours of mechanical breakdowns upfront, leaving only simple arithmetic for the heavy hourly loop.
