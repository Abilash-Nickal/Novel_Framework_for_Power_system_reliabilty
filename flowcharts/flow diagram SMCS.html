Key Innovation

Using -MTTF * np.log(rand) ensures the simulation follows the Markov Memoryless Property, accurately representing random mechanical failures without tracking historical downtime.

Island Adaptation

The min(1.0, ...) ensures that even if a generator doesn't fail for days, we still step forward and check the load profile every hour to detect peaks.
